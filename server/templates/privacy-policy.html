Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

PaceUp is built to help you track your runs and rides and connect with others. We collect only what we need to make the app work, and we never sell your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account and use PaceUp, we collect the following:

  • Account information – your name and email address
  • Location data – precise GPS location while you are actively tracking a run or ride
  • Fitness data – distance, pace, duration, elevation, and mile splits for each activity
  • Photos – images you choose to attach to your activity posts
  • User content – posts, comments, reactions, crew details, and any other content you create in the app
  • User ID – an internal account identifier used to link your data together

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to:

  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • Record and display your activity history, splits, and performance rankings
  • Power social features including the activity feed, crews, and friend connections
  • Personalise your experience — goal tracking, pace comparisons, and suggested routes
  • Send notifications you have opted in to receive
  • Diagnose crashes and improve app performance

3. Location Data

PaceUp requests access to your precise location only while tracking an activity. Location data is used solely to record your route and calculate distance and pace. We do not track your location in the background unless you have enabled background tracking for active run sessions.

You can revoke location permission at any time through your device settings. Doing so will disable GPS-based activity tracking.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with third-party advertisers or data brokers. Your data may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Other users – activity posts and profile information you choose to share publicly within the app are visible to other PaceUp users
  • Service providers – infrastructure providers (hosting, databases) that process data on our behalf under confidentiality agreements
  • Legal requirements – if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data — including your activity history, posts, photos, and profile — is permanently deleted within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent for optional data collection at any time

To delete your account, go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account in the app, or visit paceupapp.com/delete-account.

7. Children's Privacy

PaceUp is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorised access.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of PaceUp after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
