Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your PaceUp account and all associated data directly from within the app.

Delete from the app

Follow these steps to delete your account:

  1. 1 Open PaceUp and go to your Profile tab
  2. 2 Tap the Settings icon in the top right
  3. 3 Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  4. 4 Confirm the deletion when prompted
This action is permanent. Deleting your account will remove all your runs, routes, achievements, friends, and crew memberships. This cannot be undone.

Need help?

If you're unable to access the app or need assistance, contact us and we'll delete your account manually within 30 days.
